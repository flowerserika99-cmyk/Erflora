Studio philosophy

Flowers that feel like they belong to your story.

Every arrangement at Erflora begins with people, not palettes. Erika’s background in literature and travel shapes how she listens to your story and translates it into flowers—pulling from seasonal stems, subtle textures, and small, layered details that reward a second look.

Weddings & celebrations

From intimate city hall ceremonies to full weekend celebrations, we design florals that feel like an extension of your relationship, your space, and the way you actually live and gather.

Sympathy & funerals

Gentle, thoughtful pieces for moments of loss—built around your memories, favorite flowers, and the feeling you want to hold onto, rather than a template.

Weekly florals & subscriptions

Seasonal arrangements for homes, restaurants, and studios that shift alongside the markets and the weather, bringing a quiet sense of rhythm to your space.
