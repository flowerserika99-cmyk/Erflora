About Erflora
An intentional studio, not a production line.

Erika graduated from Princeton University with a passion for nature, and despite a traditional career path, she decided to go an indpendent path.

With an eye for detail, she saw how flowers showed up in the most important points of life. Meaningful in purpose and practice, she focused on the details of botany.

Erflora is the result: a studio where designs are seasonal, foam-free, and personal. Every piece is made to order, with the kind of attention you can only give when you’ve chosen this work on purpose.
