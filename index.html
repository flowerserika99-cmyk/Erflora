About Erika & Erflora

A studio built from study, travel, and a love of quiet details.

Erika studied at Princeton, but her favorite lessons came outside the classroom — in greenhouses, flower markets, and train stations while traveling. Rather than step into a predictable path, she chose a studio life built around color, texture, and the stories people tell through flowers.

Erflora began as a small practice making bouquets for friends and family, evolving into a studio that now supports weddings, memorials, anniversaries, and small-scale events. Every arrangement is rooted in seasonality, using blooms that feel natural to the moment rather than forced or overly staged.

The studio works with a limited number of events at a time so that each design can be approached slowly and intentionally — from a single stem on a bedside table to a ceremony installation.

Erflora is ideal for couples and families who:

• Prefer seasonal flowers over strict color “themes”.

• Enjoy designs that feel garden-inspired, airy, and textural.

• Value intentional details over maximal scale.

• Want to work directly with the designer, not an order form.

Services

Every project begins with a short conversation and a simple proposal.

Weddings & Elopements

Custom florals for ceremonies, bridal bouquets, personals, and intimate receptions. Designs are fully tailored to your venue, season, and comfort with color.

Typical investment: starting around $1,800 for small weddings.

Memorial & Sympathy Florals

Gentle, thoughtful arrangements for memorial services, celebrations of life, and family gatherings — with attention to favorite flowers, colors, and stories.

Typical investment: from $350 for coordinated pieces.

Anniversaries & Personal Bouquets

One-of-a-kind bouquets and arrangements for anniversaries, birthdays, and meaningful milestones, delivered within the studio’s service area.

Typical investment: from $125 per arrangement.

Editorial & Small Events

Florals for photo shoots, micro-events, and creative collaborations. Limited availability; ideal for slower, more experimental projects.

Pricing by proposal based on scope.

Every event and arrangement is priced individually. After you share your date, location, and a few preferences, you’ll receive a simple proposal outlining recommended pieces, a price range, and how flowers can best support the tone of your day.

The studio does not currently offer a public “menu” or online shop — everything begins with a brief email and a few questions.
